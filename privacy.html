Information We Collect

When you interact with Lorvien Parfums, we may collect different types of personal and technical information depending on your activity on our website.

Personal Information

We may collect your full name, mobile number, email address (if provided), billing address, shipping address, and other information required to process and deliver your order.

Order Information

When you place an order, we collect details about the products purchased, order value, payment status, delivery information, and communication related to your purchase.

Device Information

We may automatically collect technical information such as your browser type, operating system, device type, IP address, language preference, pages visited, and approximate location for security, analytics, and website performance improvements.

Communication Information

If you contact us through WhatsApp, email, Instagram, or other communication channels, we may retain those conversations to provide customer support and improve our services.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to operate our business efficiently and provide a smooth shopping experience.

  • Process and confirm your orders.
  • Arrange shipping and delivery.
  • Provide customer support.
  • Respond to enquiries.
  • Improve our products and services.
  • Prevent fraud and unauthorized activities.
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Lorvien Parfums may use cookies and similar technologies to improve website performance, remember user preferences, understand visitor behaviour, and enhance the overall browsing experience. Cookies do not normally contain personally identifiable information, but they may be associated with information you have voluntarily provided to us.

You may disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. However, certain features of the website may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

Payment Information

Lorvien Parfums currently accepts prepaid orders only. We do not store your debit card, credit card, UPI PIN, banking passwords, or other sensitive payment credentials. Payments are processed through trusted payment methods or service providers.

Third-Party Services

To operate our business efficiently, we may use trusted third-party services including shipping partners, payment service providers, website hosting services, analytics providers, and communication platforms. These service providers receive only the information necessary to perform their respective services.

Shipping Partners

When your order is shipped, your name, phone number, and delivery address may be shared with our logistics partners solely for the purpose of delivering your order and providing shipment updates.

Marketing Communications

If you voluntarily contact us or subscribe to promotional updates, we may occasionally send information regarding new fragrance launches, exclusive offers, or important announcements. You may opt out of promotional communications at any time by contacting us.

Data Retention

We retain customer information only for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfil orders, provide customer support, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and maintain business records. Information that is no longer required may be securely deleted or anonymised.

Data Security

Lorvien Parfums implements reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect customer information from unauthorised access, misuse, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. While no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, we continuously work to safeguard your personal information using industry-standard practices.
