Introduction

Thank you for choosing Lorvien Parfums. We are committed to processing and delivering every order safely and efficiently. This Shipping Policy explains how orders are processed, shipped, and delivered when you purchase products from our website.

By placing an order with Lorvien Parfums, you agree to the terms outlined in this Shipping Policy.

Order Processing

All orders are processed after successful payment confirmation. Orders are usually prepared for dispatch within 1–2 business days. Orders placed on Sundays or public holidays will be processed on the next working day.

Shipping Coverage

Lorvien Parfums currently delivers to most serviceable locations across India. Delivery availability depends on the service coverage of our courier partners.

Estimated Delivery Time

Estimated delivery times are as follows:

  • Kerala – 2 to 5 Business Days
  • South India – 3 to 6 Business Days
  • Rest of India – 4 to 8 Business Days

These delivery estimates may vary due to weather conditions, courier delays, natural disasters, public holidays, or other unforeseen circumstances.

Shipping Charges

Shipping charges, if applicable, will be displayed during checkout before payment is completed. Promotional offers such as free shipping may be available during selected campaigns.

Order Tracking

Once your order has been dispatched, shipment details and tracking information (where available) will be shared through WhatsApp or other official communication channels.

Delivery Attempts

Our courier partners may attempt delivery multiple times. If delivery cannot be completed due to an incorrect address, unavailable recipient, or incorrect contact information, the shipment may be returned to us. Additional shipping charges may apply for re-dispatch.

Address Accuracy

Customers are responsible for providing accurate delivery information. Lorvien Parfums is not responsible for delays or failed deliveries resulting from incorrect or incomplete addresses provided during checkout.

Damaged or Lost Shipments

If your package arrives visibly damaged or appears to be lost during transit, please contact us immediately through our official WhatsApp number. We will coordinate with our courier partner to investigate the issue and provide an appropriate resolution where applicable.

Shipping Delays

Although we strive to deliver every order within the estimated timeframe, unexpected delays may occasionally occur. Lorvien Parfums shall not be held responsible for delays caused by courier services, weather conditions, government restrictions, strikes, or other events beyond our reasonable control.
